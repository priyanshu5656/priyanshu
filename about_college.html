Education is the key to empower girls.

This college is a college that marked its step over whole district in women's education. It was founded by Prof. Baliram Mishra. While studying
he saw the plight of girls and worried about their education.On that time there were no any proper platform for females educations, so he
thought to give a good platform to the girls by making an special institution for them.He set up an instituion specially for girls named as "Nagendra Jha Mahila
College".He ,intially, started the institution from five to six rooms. There were no proper infrastructure and facilities.
It emphasis on life skill activities, providing students an edge over others to be leaders in their respective field of activity. Through life skills programme,
the school aims to, develop a cadre of empowered, educated and healthy adolescents, capable of making decisions in life.
Early his father has denoted his land for the purpose of setting a primary school in his own native village.
This immense institution is being settled by the blessing of his father(Late Yamuna Mishra) and her mother (Late Lakhrajo Devi).

Now this instituion has changed and it tranformed into four-Storeyed building. Also its number of students rose to 5,000 and still counting.
